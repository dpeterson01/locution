## Locution 0.1.45

Recording overlay placement fixes.

## What's new

- **The recording bar shows up reliably** — fixed a bug where the bar could fail to appear at its set position (for example, at the bottom of the screen) until you toggled the position setting off and on. It now places itself correctly on its own.
- **Placement survives display changes and sleep** — the bar re-centers on the correct screen edge after you change displays or wake your Mac from sleep, instead of getting stranded in the wrong spot.

## Requirements

- macOS or Windows
- [Ollama](https://ollama.com) running locally for AI cleanup (`qwen2.5:3b` for quick cleanup and `qwen2.5:14b` for longer dictation)

Built on Handy. Thanks to the Handy project, Whisper, and Ollama.
