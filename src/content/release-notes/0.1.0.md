## Locution 0.1.0

The first public release of Locution, a local-first macOS dictation app forked from [Handy](https://github.com/cjpais/Handy).

Speak naturally and get clean, formatted text pasted into any app. Transcription runs locally with Whisper; cleanup runs locally with Ollama. Nothing leaves your machine.

## What's here

- **Modes** — pick how your dictation comes out (Clean up, Message, Email, Note, Rewrite). Each mode is a complete recipe for edit strength, tone, and format.
- **Voice** — a personal style card plus built-in guardrails, so cleanup sounds like you rather than like a press release.
- **Per-app auto-mode** — optionally let the app you're typing in choose your mode automatically.
- **Snippets** — short triggers expand to boilerplate, inserted verbatim.
- **Adaptive cleanup** — short dictation returns fast; longer dictation is cleaned more thoroughly.

## Requirements

- macOS
- [Ollama](https://ollama.com) running locally for AI cleanup (models `phi4-mini:latest` and `gemma3:12b`)

Built on Handy. Thanks to the Handy project, Whisper, and Ollama.
