## Locution 0.1.46

Dictation that spells the people you're writing to.

## What's new

- **Recipient-aware names** — when you dictate into Teams, Outlook, or Messages,
  Locution reads who the message is addressed to and uses those names to spell
  them correctly, including on the quick cleanup tier. Turn it on under
  Settings → Cleanup → Screen context capture.
- **Keeps names you already typed** — if you fix a name in the box and then keep
  dictating, cleanup preserves your spelling instead of overwriting it. Runs on
  the context-enabled cleanup tier.
- **Recording bar placement fixes** — the bar appears reliably at its set
  position and re-centers correctly after display changes or waking from sleep.
- **Ollama starts with cleanup** — when local AI cleanup is on, Ollama starts at
  login so the first dictation isn't waiting on a cold start.

## Requirements

- macOS or Windows
- [Ollama](https://ollama.com) running locally for AI cleanup (`qwen2.5:3b` for
  quick cleanup and `qwen2.5:14b` for longer dictation)
